Zohran Mamdani Policy Impact Simulator

This project simulates the economic impact of two policy ideas associated with Zohran Mamdani:

  • Rent Freeze – estimates tenant savings and landlord revenue loss.
  • Free Buses – estimates passenger savings, MTA ticket revenue loss, and taxi driver income loss.

Methodology

Using simple parameter-based models in Python, the simulator applies assumptions such as: number of units, average rents, daily trips, fares, and behaviour shifts from taxis to buses. It then computes estimated gains and losses and visualises them with charts.
