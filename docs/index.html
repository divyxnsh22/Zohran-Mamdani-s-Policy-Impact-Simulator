NYC Policy Impact Simulator

This project models the economic impact of two policy ideas proposed by Zohran Mamdani:

  • Rent Freeze – estimating tenant savings and landlord revenue loss
  • Free Buses – estimating passenger savings, MTA ticket revenue loss, and taxi driver income loss

Methodology

The model uses parameter-based simulation to estimate financial impact using assumptions such as: average rent, number of housing units, daily bus trips, average fares, and behavioural shifts from taxis to buses.
